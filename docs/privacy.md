# Privacy Policy — Vogue WOD Schedule

_Last updated: 2026-05-27_

Vogue WOD Schedule ("the app") is an unofficial workout-schedule reader
for Vogue Fitness UAE. This policy explains what the app does and does
not do with data. It is written to be honest and simple because the app
is simple.

## The short version

**The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**
There are no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no
tracking.

## What the app stores

Everything below is stored **only on your device** and never leaves it:

- **Your favorite branch** — the gym location you pick on first launch,
  so the app can open to it.
- **A cached copy of the workout schedule** — so the app works offline
  and loads instantly.
- **Your training log** — any WODs you have marked done, with optional
  score and notes.

You can erase all of it at any time by deleting the app.

## Network activity

To show the schedule, the app makes standard HTTPS requests to
**`vfuae.com`** to download the public "Workout of the Day" page. These
requests:

- send no personal information — only a normal request for a public page;
- are made directly from your device to that website;
- are the only network connections the app makes.

The `vfuae.com` website is operated by Vogue Fitness, a third party.
Their handling of any standard web-server logs is governed by their own
policies, not this one.

## Third parties

The app contains **no** third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising,
crash reporting, or tracking.

The app uses **Google Fonts** to download two display typefaces
(Bodoni Moda, Inter, JetBrains Mono) on first launch and caches them on
your device. This fetch is a routine HTTPS request and is governed by
[Google Fonts' privacy notice](https://developers.google.com/fonts/faq/privacy).

## Children

The app is a general-audience schedule viewer and does not knowingly
collect data from anyone, including children.

## Permissions

- **Internet access** — used solely to download the public schedule and
  the display typefaces.

## Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change, this document will be updated
and the "Last updated" date above will change.

## Affiliation

Vogue WOD Schedule is **not affiliated with, or endorsed by, Vogue
Fitness**. The brand name is used descriptively to identify whose
schedule is shown; all workout content is the work of their coaches.
