# Vogue WOD Schedule

An unofficial daily-workout schedule reader for **Vogue Fitness UAE**
branches. Pick your home branch once; the app opens straight to today's
WOD. Works offline; includes a built-in workout timer and a personal
training log.

- [Privacy Policy](privacy/)
- [Source on GitHub](https://github.com/tp9imka/vogue)

_Not affiliated with, or endorsed by, Vogue Fitness. The brand name is
used descriptively to identify whose schedule is shown; all workout
content is the work of their coaches. Schedule data is sourced from the
public page at <https://vfuae.com/wod/>._
